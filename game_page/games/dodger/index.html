How To Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move. You can only move discretely to each box. Laser warning ! will appear to indicate an incoming laser. After the wait, if you are in line with it, you are hit and die. The lasers come from the top and the side and speed up as time passes. The point of the game is to see how long you can last.
