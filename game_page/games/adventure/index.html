How To Play

Use AD to move left and right. Use SPACEBAR to jump, holding makes you go higher. Use LEFT CLICK to dash in the direcion you're moving. If you leave the screen on the right, you appear on the left and vice versa. Jump up the screen with platforms and there will be a goal at the end. If you reach it, the level will reset, but this time the goal is higher, the speeds of the platforms vary more and so do their sizes. See what record you can get. I got to level 5 :).
